 The statistic shows the gross domestic product (GDP) per capita in Zambia from 1994 to 2019, with projections up until 2024. GDP is the total value of all goods and services produced in a country in a year. It is considered to be a very important indicator of the economic strength of a country. In 2019, the GDP per capita in Zambia amounted to around 859.95 U.S. dollars.
