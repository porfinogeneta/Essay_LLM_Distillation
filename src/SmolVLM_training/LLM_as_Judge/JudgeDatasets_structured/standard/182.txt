 Social media and news websites are the most common pathways to online news. When asked how they got news in the past two hours, 36% of online news consumers said they got it through a news organization’s website or app, while 35% said they got it through social media. Search engines were the third most common way to get news, with 20% of respondents saying they used them. News organizations’ email, text, or alert services were used by 15% of respondents, while 7% said they used family or friend email or text. The remaining 9% of respondents said they used other methods to get news.
