 Mobile phone owners in sub-Saharan Africa are more likely to use their phones to send text messages, take pictures or video, access social networking sites and make or receive payments than their counterparts in other regions.
In the past 12 months, 78% of mobile phone owners in sub-Saharan Africa have used their phones to send text messages, compared with 59% in South Asia, 42% in Latin America and 35% in Europe.
In the past 12 months, 59% of mobile phone owners in sub-Saharan Africa have used their phones to access social networking sites, compared with 42% in South Asia, 30% in Latin America and 35% in Europe.
In the past 12 months, 59% of mobile phone owners in sub-Saharan Africa have used their phones to make or receive payments, compared with 30% in South Asia, 25% in Latin America and 25% in Europe.
