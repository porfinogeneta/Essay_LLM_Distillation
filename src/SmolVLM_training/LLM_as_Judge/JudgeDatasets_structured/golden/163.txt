Most Americans have never spoken with a local journalist
1. <Describe a graph:>
The pie chart illustrates the percentage of U.S. adults who have ever spoken with or been interviewed by a local journalist, based on a survey conducted from October 15 to November 8, 2018.

2. <Find the key points and trends:>
The majority of Americans, 78%, have never spoken with a local journalist. In contrast, only 21% have had such an interaction. A small fraction, 1%, did not provide an answer.

3. <Write a summary of presented data:>
Overall, the data indicates a significant gap between those who have and have not engaged with local journalists, highlighting a potential disconnect between the public and local media.
