 1. <Describe the trend of the production of biofuel in the United States from 2010 to 2019>:
The production of biofuel in the United States has been increasing since 2010. In 2019, the production was at 1,557.1 petajoules, up from 1,43.4 petajoules in 2010. The production has been consistently increasing over the years, with a notable increase in 2019.
