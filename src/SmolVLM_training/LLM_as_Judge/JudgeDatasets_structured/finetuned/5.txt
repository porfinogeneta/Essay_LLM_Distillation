2. <Find the key points, trends and compare data>:

Paragraph 1:
"The data reveals a consistent upward trend in the average waiting time for gallstone surgery over the five-year period. Starting at 73.2 days in 2010, the waiting time gradually increased to 80.6 days in 2011. This upward trend continued, with the waiting time reaching 73.8 days in 2012 and 73.1 days in 2013.
