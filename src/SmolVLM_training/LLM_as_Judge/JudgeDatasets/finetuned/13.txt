 1. <Describe the trend in the number of fatal crashes in the United States by month in 2018 ( per 100 million vehicle miles traveled )>:

The number of fatal crashes in the United States by month in 2018 ( per 100 million vehicle miles traveled ) shows a general upward trend from January to December. The highest number of fatal crashes was recorded in September, with 1.13 crashes per 100 million vehicle miles traveled. The lowest number of fatal crashes was recorded in February, with 0.93 crashes per 100 million vehicle miles traveled.
