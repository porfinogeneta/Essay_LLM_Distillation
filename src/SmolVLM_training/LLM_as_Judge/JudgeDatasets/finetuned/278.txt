 1. <Describe the trend of the net income of Barrick Gold from 2008 to 2019>:
The net income of Barrick Gold fluctuated over the years. In 2008, the net income was at its lowest point, reaching -4,274 million U.S. dollars. However, it increased significantly in the following years, reaching 4,666 million U.S. dollars in 2011. The net income then declined again, reaching -2,74 million U.S. dollars in 2012. From 2013 onwards, the net income gradually increased, reaching 818 million U.S. dollars in 2016. The net income further increased in the subsequent years, reaching 902 million U.S. dollars in 2019.
