Paragraph 1:
"A significant majority, 56%, believe that the media's coverage of the healthcare law has been mostly about politics and controversies. In contrast, only 6% think it has been mostly about how the law might impact people."

"A third of respondents, 33%, feel that the coverage has been a balance of both politics and the law's impact on people. Additionally, 5% of respondents either didn't know or refused to answer the question."
