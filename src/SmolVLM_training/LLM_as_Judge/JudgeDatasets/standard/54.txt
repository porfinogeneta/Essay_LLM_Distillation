 The number of U.S. employees for U.S. affiliates of foreign multinational enterprises has increased by nearly 7 million since 2007, according to preliminary data from the U.S. Bureau of Economic Analysis. In 2015, 6.82 million U.S. employees were working for U.S. affiliates of foreign multinational enterprises, up from 5.58 million in 2007.
The number of U.S. employees for U.S. affiliates of foreign multinational enterprises has increased by more than 1.25 million since 2007, or about 2.25 percentage points.
