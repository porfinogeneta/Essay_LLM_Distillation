 Republicans are increasingly critical of the U.S. Supreme Court, with 50% saying it is liberal, up from 22% in 2007. The share of Republicans who say the court is liberal has increased by 28 percentage points since 2007.
The survey also finds that Republicans are increasingly critical of the court’s decision to strike down the health care law. In July 2012, 41% of Republicans said the court’s decision to strike down the health care law was a good thing for the country, while 39% said it was a bad thing. By July 2013, 50% of Republicans said the court’s decision was a bad thing, while 22% said it was a good thing.
