 The acceptance rate of 10 largest asylum applicant groups in the first half of 2015 was 94.8% for Syrians, the highest of any group. The acceptance rate for Syrians was 88.5% for Eritreans, 87.1% for Iraqis, 69.4% for Afghans, 61.9% for Somalians, 27.1% for Pakistanis, 25.8% for Ukrainians, 6.4% for Albanians and 1.9% for Kosovars.
