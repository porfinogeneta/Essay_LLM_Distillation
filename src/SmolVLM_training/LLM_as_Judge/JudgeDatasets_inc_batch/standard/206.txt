 Adults who grew up in rural areas are more likely than those who grew up in other areas to say there was a gun in the household when they were growing up. About seven-in-ten adults who grew up in rural areas (72%) say there was a gun in the household when they were growing up, compared with 48% of all adults.
The share of adults who say there was a gun in the household when they were growing up is higher in small towns (52%) and suburbs (37%) than in cities (39%).
