 Online task platforms are a popular way for people to make money, but many users say they are also a way to pass the time. About half of gig platform workers (42%) say they use the platforms to have fun or do something with their spare time.
The next most common reason for using gig platforms is to help fill in gaps or deal with fluctuations in income (37%). About a quarter of workers (20%) say they use the platforms to gain future job experience.
