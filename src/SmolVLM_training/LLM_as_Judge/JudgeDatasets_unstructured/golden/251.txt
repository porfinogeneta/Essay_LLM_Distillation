Online share of the global FMCG market worldwide from 2017 to 2025

The bar chart illustrates the online share of the global FMCG market worldwide from 2017 to 2025, with projections for the future.

The chart reveals a steady increase in the online share of the global FMCG market over the years. Starting at 4.3% in 2017, the share rose to 5.1% in 2018. The trend indicates a significant upward trajectory, with projections estimating a substantial rise to 10% by 2025.

This growth reflects the increasing consumer preference for online shopping, driven by factors such as convenience, wider product selection, and competitive pricing. The rise in e-commerce platforms and digital payment systems has further facilitated this shift.

Overall, the data underscores a robust growth in the online FMCG market, highlighting a clear trend towards digitalization in consumer purchasing behavior. The projected 10% share by 2025 signifies the expanding influence of online retail in the global FMCG sector.
