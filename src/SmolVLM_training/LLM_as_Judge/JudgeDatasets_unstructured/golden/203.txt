Share of haunted house visitors in the United States between 2014 and 2016 , by age group

The bar chart illustrates the share of haunted house visitors in the United States between 2014 and 2016, categorized by age group.

The data reveals that the highest proportion of haunted house visitors falls within the 18-24 age group, with a significant 25% share. This is followed by the 25-34 age group, which accounts for 19.6% of the visitors. The 35-44 age group also shows a notable presence, comprising 16.9% of the total visitors.

In contrast, the older age groups exhibit a considerably lower share of visitors. The 45-54 age group represents 11.8% of the visitors, while the 55-64 age group accounts for only 6.4%. The 65+ age group has the smallest share, with a mere 2.3% of the visitors.

Overall, the data indicates a clear trend where younger age groups are more inclined to visit haunted houses, with the 18-24 age group leading the way. The share of visitors decreases progressively with age, highlighting a strong preference for haunted house experiences among younger demographics.
