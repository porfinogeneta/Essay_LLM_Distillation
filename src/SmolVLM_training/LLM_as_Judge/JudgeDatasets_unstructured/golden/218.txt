Music publishing revenue of the Universal Music Group from 2007 to 2019 ( in million euros )

The bar chart illustrates the music publishing revenue of the Universal Music Group from 2007 to 2019, measured in million euros.

The data reveals a general upward trend in revenue over the twelve-year period. Starting at 589 million euros in 2007, the revenue experienced a steady increase, reaching 662 million euros by 2010. This growth continued, albeit with minor fluctuations, culminating in a significant peak of 1,168 million euros in 2019.

Between 2010 and 2015, the revenue growth was relatively modest, with figures hovering around 655 to 756 million euros. However, from 2016 onwards, there was a marked acceleration in revenue growth. The revenue surged from 792 million euros in 2016 to 854 million euros in 2017, and then to 941 million euros in 2018, before reaching its zenith in 2019.

Overall, the Universal Music Groups music publishing revenue demonstrated a robust and consistent increase from 2007 to 2019. The most notable growth occurred in the latter part of the period, particularly between 2016 and 2019, highlighting a significant upward trajectory in the companys financial performance.
