Handling Most Latinos Disapprove of Obama S 
A significant majority of Latinos disapprove of Obamas handling of deportations, with 59% expressing disapproval. In contrast, only 27% approve of his approach.

A smaller portion, 13%, either did not know or refused to answer the question, indicating a degree of uncertainty or lack of opinion on the matter.

Overall, the data reveals a strong sentiment of disapproval among Latinos towards Obamas handling of deportations, with a clear majority expressing negative views and a smaller fraction showing approval or uncertainty.
