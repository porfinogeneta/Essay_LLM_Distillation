Advertiser expenditure for evening network news
The graph illustrates the gross advertiser expenditures for network TV evening news programs from 2013 to 2018.

In 2013, the gross advertiser expenditures for network TV evening news programs were approximately $500 million. This figure saw a gradual increase over the next few years, peaking at around $600 million in 2016.

However, starting in 2017, there was a noticeable decline in advertiser spending. By 2018, the expenditures had dropped back to the 2013 level of approximately $500 million.

Overall, the data indicates a period of growth in advertiser spending on network TV evening news programs from 2013 to 2016, followed by a decline, bringing the expenditures back to the initial level by 2018.
