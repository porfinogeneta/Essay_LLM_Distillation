Adjusted profit before tax of the British Barclays group from 2013 to 2019 ( in million GBP )

The bar chart illustrates the adjusted profit before tax of the British Barclays group from 2013 to 2019, measured in million GBP.

The data reveals a fluctuating trend in the adjusted profit before tax of Barclays over the given period. Starting at 1,879 million GBP in 2013, the profit experienced a decline in the subsequent years, reaching its lowest point at 1,146 million GBP in 2015.

However, from 2016 onwards, there is a noticeable upward trajectory. The profit increased significantly to 3,230 million GBP in 2016 and continued to rise, peaking at 4,357 million GBP in 2019. This represents a substantial growth from the initial figure in 2013.

Overall, despite an initial decline, Barclays adjusted profit before tax demonstrated robust growth from 2016 to 2019, reflecting a positive recovery and expansion in the latter part of the decade.
