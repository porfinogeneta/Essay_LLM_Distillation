Market share of the leading wireless headphone vendors as of 3rd quarter 2019

The bar chart illustrates the market share of the leading wireless headphone vendors as of the third quarter of 2019. The vendors included are JBL, Sony, and Bose.

JBL holds the largest market share among the three vendors, accounting for 13%. This is followed by Sony, which holds a 10% market share. Bose, on the other hand, has the smallest market share at 8%.

The data indicates that JBL is the most dominant player in the wireless headphone market as of the third quarter of 2019. Sony, while trailing behind JBL, still maintains a significant presence in the market. Bose, although having the smallest share among the three, remains a notable competitor.

In summary, JBL leads the wireless headphone market with a 13% share, followed by Sony with 10%, and Bose with 8%. The data highlights the competitive nature of the market, with JBL holding a slight edge over its competitors.
