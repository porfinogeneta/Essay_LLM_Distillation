Overwhelming Discontent
The pie chart illustrates the overwhelming discontent among the population regarding the current state of affairs in the country, based on a survey conducted in Spring 2014.

An overwhelming majority of 81% of respondents expressed dissatisfaction with the way things are going in the country. This sentiment is starkly contrasted by the mere 17% who reported being satisfied. Additionally, a small fraction of 2% of respondents were unsure or did not have an opinion on the matter.

Overall, the data indicates a profound level of discontent among the population, with a significant majority expressing dissatisfaction and only a small fraction feeling satisfied or uncertain about the current state of affairs in the country.
