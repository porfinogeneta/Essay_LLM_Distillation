Minnesota Vikings franchise value from 2002 to 2020 ( in million U.S. dollars ) 

The bar chart illustrates the franchise value of the Minnesota Vikings from 2002 to 2020, measured in million U.S. dollars.

The franchise value of the Minnesota Vikings has demonstrated a consistent upward trend from 2002 to 2020. Starting at $437 million in 2002, the value has progressively increased each year, reaching $2,950 million by 2020.

The most notable increments occurred in the latter part of the period. Between 2014 and 2015, the value surged from $1,150 million to $1,590 million, marking a significant jump. Similarly, the period from 2018 to 2020 saw substantial growth, with the value rising from $2,700 million to $2,950 million.

This consistent increase in franchise value reflects the growing popularity and financial success of the NFL, as well as the strategic management and marketability of the Minnesota Vikings.

Overall, the franchise value of the Minnesota Vikings has shown a remarkable upward trajectory over the 18-year period, underscoring the teams growing financial stature and the broader prosperity of the NFL.
