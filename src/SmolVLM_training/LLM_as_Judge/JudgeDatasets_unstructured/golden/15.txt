The pie chart illustrates the perceptions of Indians regarding their countrys status as a world power, based on a survey conducted by the Pew Research Center.

A significant majority of respondents, 49%, believe that India will eventually become a world power. This sentiment is closely followed by 38% who already consider India to be a world power. A small fraction, 8%, thinks that India will never achieve world power status, while 6% are unsure or have no opinion on the matter.

The data indicates a strong sense of optimism and confidence among Indians about their countrys future on the global stage. The belief that India will eventually become a world power is the most prevalent view, suggesting a forward-looking perspective among the population.

Overall, the majority of Indians are optimistic about their countrys future, with a significant portion already considering India a world power and an even larger group believing it will eventually become one. A small minority doubts Indias potential to achieve this status.
