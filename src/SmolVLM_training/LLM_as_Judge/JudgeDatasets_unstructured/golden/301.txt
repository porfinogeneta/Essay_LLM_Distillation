Number of social network users in the Philippines from 2017 to 2019 with a forecast until 2025 ( in millions )

The bar chart illustrates the number of social network users in the Philippines from 2017 to 2019, with a forecast extending to 2025. The figures are presented in millions.

The data reveals a consistent upward trend in the number of social network users in the Philippines over the given period. Starting at 67.2 million users in 2017, the number increased to 72.38 million in 2018 and further to 76.29 million in 2019.

Looking ahead, the forecast predicts continued growth, with the number of users expected to reach 79.58 million in 2020 and 82.33 million in 2021. By 2025, the number of social network users is projected to soar to 90.04 million, indicating a robust expansion in social media usage.

Overall, the data indicates a significant and steady increase in social network users in the Philippines from 2017 to 2025. The number of users is expected to grow from 67.2 million in 2017 to 90.04 million in 2025, reflecting the rising popularity and penetration of social media platforms in the country.
