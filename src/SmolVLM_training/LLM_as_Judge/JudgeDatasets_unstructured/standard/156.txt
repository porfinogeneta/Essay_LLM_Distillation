 This bar graph is titled Lead Newsmakers. The x-axis measures Lead Newsmakers while the y-axis plots Number of Stories. The chart shows the number of stories about five different newsmakers in the week of July 26th to August 1st, 2010. Barack Obama was the most newsworthy newsmaker with 70 stories. The second most newsworthy newsmaker was Charles Rangel with 30 stories. The least newsworthy newsmaker was Anthony Weiner with 10 stories.
