 The bar chart illustrates the share of households with internet access in Romania from 2007 to 2018.

In 2007, the share of households with internet access in Romania was 22%. This figure increased to 30% in 2008 and further rose to 38% in 2009. By 2010, the share had further increased to 42%, and it continued to rise in subsequent years. In 2011, the share of households with internet access in Romania was 47%, and it reached 54% in 2012.

From 2013 onwards, the share of households with internet access in Romania continued to rise, reaching 61% in 2014. This trend continued in subsequent years, with the share increasing to 68% in 2015, 72% in 2016, and 76% in 2017. By 2018, the share of households with internet access in Romania had reached 81%, indicating a significant increase in internet penetration over the decade.

Overall, the data reveals a consistent upward trend in the share of households with internet access in Romania from 2007 to 2018. The growth rate was particularly rapid in the early years, with the share increasing by over 10% in each subsequent year. However, the rate of increase slowed down in the later years, with the share increasing by less than 5% from 2017 to 2018.
