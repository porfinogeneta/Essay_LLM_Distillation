 The bar chart illustrates the percentage of U.S. adults who use Twitter as of February 2019, by age group. The data reveals that the highest usage rate of Twitter is among the 18-29 age group, with 38% of respondents using the platform. This is followed by the 30-49 age group, with 26% of respondents using Twitter. The usage rate drops significantly for the 50-64 age group, with only 17% of respondents using Twitter. The lowest usage rate is observed in the 65+ age group, with only 7% of respondents using Twitter. Overall, the data indicates that younger generations are more likely to use Twitter compared to older generations.
