 The bar chart illustrates the music publishing revenue of the Universal Music Group from 2007 to 2019, measured in million euros.

The revenue of music publishing for Universal Music Group has shown a significant upward trend over the years. In 2007, the revenue was at 589 million euros, which increased to 648 million euros in 2008. The revenue continued to rise, reaching 659 million euros in 2009.

From 2010 onwards, the revenue experienced a slight decline, with 662 million euros in 2010, 638 million euros in 2011, and 661 million euros in 2012. However, the revenue rebounded in 2013, reaching 655 million euros.

From 2014 onwards, the revenue has consistently increased, with 673 million euros in 2014, 756 million euros in 2015, and 792 million euros in 2016. The revenue further rose to 854 million euros in 2017, and then to 941 million euros in 2018.

In 2019, the revenue of music publishing for Universal Music Group reached its highest point, at 1,168 million euros. This indicates a substantial growth in revenue from 2007 to 2019, highlighting the company's success in the music publishing industry.
