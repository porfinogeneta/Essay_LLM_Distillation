 The pie chart illustrates the views of Indians regarding their country's power in the world, as per a survey conducted in 2005.

The data reveals that a significant majority of Indians (49%) believe that India will eventually become a world power, while a smaller proportion (38%) anticipate already being a world power. Only 6% of respondents expect India to never be a world power, and 8% are unsure.

Interestingly, the survey also indicates that a considerable portion of Indians (6%) are undecided about India's future power status. This suggests a degree of uncertainty or indecision among the population regarding India's global standing.

Overall, the data indicates a strong belief among Indians that their country will eventually become a world power, with a significant minority anticipating this outcome. However, a notable minority remains unsure or undecided, highlighting the complexities and evolving perceptions of India's global power trajectory.
