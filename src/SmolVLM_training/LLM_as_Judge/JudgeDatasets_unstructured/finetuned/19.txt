 The bar chart illustrates the number of companies in Italy as of September 2019, by region. Lombardy leads with the highest number of companies at 961,683, followed by Lazio with 661,794. Veneto and Sicily are the third and fourth most populous regions, with 484,714 and 467,447 companies respectively. Emilia-Romagna and Piedmont are the fifth and sixth most populated regions, with 453,296 and 429,449 companies respectively. The lowest number of companies is found in the Abruzzo region, with 148,921 companies. Overall, the regions of Lombardy, Lazio, Veneto, and Sicily have the highest number of companies, while the regions of Emilia-Romagna, Piedmont, and Abruzzo have the lowest number of companies.
