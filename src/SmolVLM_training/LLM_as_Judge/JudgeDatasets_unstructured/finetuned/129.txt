 The bar chart illustrates the smartphone unit shipments in Asia Pacific from 2013 to 2018, measured in million units.

The data reveals a significant increase in smartphone shipments over the five-year period. In 2013, shipments stood at 165.2 million units, gradually rising to 213.7 million units in 2014. This upward trend continued, with shipments reaching 271.3 million units in 2015.

The following years saw a more pronounced increase, with shipments peaking at 301.2 million units in 2017. Despite a slight dip in 2018, shipments still amounted to 732 million units, indicating a robust market for smartphones in Asia Pacific.

Overall, the data highlights a consistent growth in smartphone shipments in Asia Pacific from 2013 to 2018, with the most significant increase occurring between 2015 and 2017. This trend suggests a strong consumer demand for smartphones in the region, driven by factors such as technological advancements, affordability, and the increasing popularity of mobile internet usage.
